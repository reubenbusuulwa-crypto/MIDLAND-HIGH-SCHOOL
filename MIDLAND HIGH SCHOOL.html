Welcome to Midland High School Mukono – Buntaba

Welcome to Midland High School Mukono–Buntaba, a center of excellence dedicated to nurturing young minds through quality education, strong moral values, and practical life skills. As a Seventh-day Adventist founded institution, we are committed to developing students academically, spiritually, socially, and physically. Our learning environment inspires curiosity, creativity, discipline, and leadership, preparing learners to excel in an ever-changing world. Through the dedication of our professional staff and the support of parents and stakeholders, we continue to shape responsible citizens who positively impact society. We invite you to explore our website and discover the opportunities, achievements, and vibrant school life that make Midland High School Mukono–Buntaba a place where futures are built, talents are nurtured, and dreams become reality.

About Us

Midland High School Mukono–Buntaba is a Christian institution founded on the principles and values of the Seventh-day Adventist faith. We believe that true education goes beyond academic achievement and focuses on the balanced development of the mind, body, and spirit. Guided by biblical principles, we strive to cultivate integrity, responsibility, service, and respect among our learners. Through academic excellence, spiritual growth, vocational training and character development, we empower learners to become productive citizens and future leaders.
